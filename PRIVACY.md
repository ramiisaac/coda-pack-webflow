# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how this Coda Pack ("Pack") handles information when you use it inside the Coda product.

## What the Pack does with your data

The Pack runs inside Coda. Any data you pass to the Pack (formula parameters, sync table inputs, action arguments) flows through Coda's runtime to the Pack's code and, where applicable, to the third-party APIs the Pack calls. The Pack does not collect, store, or transmit data to the maintainer.

## Authentication

If the Pack uses authentication, credentials are managed by Coda's built-in authentication system. The Pack uses the credentials only to make requests to the third-party API the Pack is built for. The Pack does not log, persist, or share credentials.

## Third-party services

When the Pack calls a third-party API, the request is sent directly from Coda's runtime (or via Coda's authentication proxy) to that third party. The third party's privacy policy governs how they handle the request and any returned data.

## Logs and telemetry

The Pack does not implement its own analytics or telemetry. Coda may collect operational logs from Pack execution as part of its normal operation; that collection is governed by [Coda's Privacy Policy](https://coda.io/policies/privacy-policy).

## Data retention

The Pack does not retain data on its own. Any data persisted while using the Pack is stored in your Coda doc (such as in sync tables you create), and retention there is governed by your own Coda workspace settings.

## Your rights

Because the Pack does not collect or store personal data, there is nothing for the maintainer to delete or export on your behalf. To manage data stored in your Coda docs, use Coda's standard doc and workspace controls.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the Pack evolves. The version that applies to your use is the version published at the URL referenced in the Pack's listing at the time of use.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the maintainer through the [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) channel listed in this repository.
